Paris was announced as one of the first five Call of Duty League franchise teams on May 2, 2019. The entire roster was announced at the same time on October 3, with Louqa, Shockz, Denz, KiSMET, Phantomz, Zed and Breszy joining. The starting roster was not yet known. On November 2, Joshh and Op2 joined and Paris became the final team to reveal their branding: Paris Legion. NovusVita joined as analyst on November 10.
