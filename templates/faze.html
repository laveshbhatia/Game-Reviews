Atlanta Faze

The team is owned and operated by Faze Gaming. Faze has been dominating the esports industry since the start of their YouTube Channel. Since then they have grown to a brand. Faze has their own apparel store which lists a new drop every month. They have partnered with giants such as nissan, champion, gfuel and many more. Faze has originated from call of duty being one of the most known teams in the call of duty world league. There were many rumours about faze not buying a spot for Activision's upcoming league.

However, the rumours were proved wrong and like every other known team in the Call of Duty League, Faze had bought in a spot and named their team as Atlanta Faze. They are expected to compete in the league and many fans think they will win couple of matches. They are based in atlanta and are looking to win all their matches. Only time will tell if they can become the next best team in call of duty. Our carefully curated team provides the full package with a roster of focused, adaptive players and a championship-winning coaching staff. The Atlanta FaZe comes to the League with the experience and ability to be fierce competition as well as a dominating force in the global gaming.
