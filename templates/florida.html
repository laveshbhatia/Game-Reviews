Florida Mutineers

Centuries ago, the sea trade routes off the coast of Florida were among the most treacherous on Earth for ships that carried precious cargo between the Old World and the New. Pirates abounded, and the harsh conditions at sea provoked many sailors to unite in mutiny and take over their overseers’ vessels to live on their own terms. Inspired by Florida’s seafaring tales of yore and the strength of Floridians in the modern era, the Florida Mutineers are relentless, unapologetic, and take no prisoners on our path to triumph.

We live, play, and fight by the Mutineers Code – a charter of rules that expresses our solidarity, our rebellious fire for victory, and the cutthroat consequences for breaking our code. Our logo features the Kraken, a legendary monster of the deep that represents the danger and mystery of the nautical life. Their roster is looking promising and they might also cement themselves in the history books of call of duty.
